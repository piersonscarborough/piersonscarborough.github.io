ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Pierson Scarborough

I'm a designer, a developer, and a creator. If you told me two years ago that I'd be a developer , I would’ve thought you were crazy. Computer Science appeared to be reserved for those who excelled in mathematics and/or science, not necessarily creatives. But as I saw the market gradually shifting towards the web and more opportunities for designers with the ability to code, I decided to dive into this challenging discipline headfirst and haven’t looked back.

The world of development is just another outlet for me to build and create. I believe there is great value in not only being able to understand design, but also being able to create and implement it through code.

When I’m not writing code, you can find me shooting photography, creating art, thrifting, and just being active outdoors. I also offer freelance design and photography on the side. Contact me and let’s build something!
